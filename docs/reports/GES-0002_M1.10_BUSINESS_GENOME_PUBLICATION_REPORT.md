## Publication Blocking Rules

### Blocking Conditions

Publication is **BLOCKED** if:

1. **Validation Failed**
   - `validationStatus === "invalid"`
   - Diagnostic: `BGC-PUB-001: VALIDATION_BLOCKS_PUBLICATION`

2. **Missing Graph**
   - Graph is null or undefined
   - Diagnostic: `BGC-PUB-002: MISSING_GRAPH`

3. **Missing Validation Result**
   - ValidationResult is null or undefined
   - Diagnostic: `BGC-PUB-003: MISSING_VALIDATION_RESULT`

4. **Graph Identity Collection Missing**
   - Required identity metadata not available
   - Diagnostic: `BGC-PUB-009: PUBLICATION_INVARIANT_VIOLATION`

### Non-Blocking Conditions

Publication **PROCEEDS** if:

1. **Validation Valid**
   - `validationStatus === "valid"`

2. **Validation Valid With Warnings**
   - `validationStatus === "valid-with-warnings"`
   - Warnings documented but do not block
