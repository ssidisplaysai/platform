# Genesis First Principles

This document captures foundational doctrine discovered so far.
It establishes constraints, not product messaging.

## What is Reality?

Reality is the state of the world independent of software representations.
Reality includes people, organizations, events, obligations, assets, and outcomes that exist whether or not they are modeled.
Software can observe, encode, and project reality, but software does not create reality itself.

## What is a Business?

A business is an organized system of purpose, actors, capabilities, obligations, and value exchange over time.
It is not equivalent to any one application, schema, or process engine.
A business has structure and behavior that can be discovered, represented, and verified.

## What is Knowledge?

Knowledge is a structured claim about reality that has sufficient evidence, context, and lineage to be trusted and reused.
Knowledge is not raw data and not unsupported assertion.
Knowledge must preserve traceability to its evidentiary basis.

## What is Evidence?

Evidence is an auditable artifact or observation that can support or refute a claim.
Evidence must be attributable to source, time, and transformation history.
Evidence without lineage is weak evidence.

## What is Truth?

Truth is the most defensible current model of reality given available evidence and verification.
Truth in Genesis is operational and auditable, not rhetorical.
Truth may evolve only through better evidence and successful verification.

## What is Software?

Software is an executable projection of selected business knowledge.
Software should implement validated understanding, not invent canonical meaning.
When software and evidence-grounded knowledge conflict, software must yield to validated knowledge.

## What is Genesis?

Genesis is a platform discipline for discovering, validating, and compiling business reality into reliable executable systems.
Genesis separates discovery of truth from projection into applications.
Its purpose is to make generated systems traceable to canonical knowledge.

## What is the Compiler?

The compiler is the mechanism that transforms evidence-grounded representations into progressively structured, validated intermediate representations.
The compiler enforces determinism, lineage preservation, and governance constraints.
It does not create truth; it operationalizes validated truth.

## Purpose of Genesis

The purpose of Genesis is to establish a repeatable path from observable business reality to trustworthy software projections.
That path requires:
- evidence before canon
- verification before generation
- lineage for every promoted claim
- deterministic compilation for auditability and reproducibility
