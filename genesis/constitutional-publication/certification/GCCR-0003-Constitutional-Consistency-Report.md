# GCCR-0003

Title: Constitutional Consistency Report
Status: Certified
Authority: Foundation Authority
Certification Date: 2026-07-21

## Scope

This report verifies the consistency of the recovered Genesis Constitutional Corpus against the constitutional governance, identity, lifecycle, authority, dependency, registry, publication, and validation framework established by GCP-0001.

## Findings

### No Conflicting Constitutional Meaning

No conflicting constitutional meaning was identified within the recovered corpus.

### No Authority Violations

No authority violations were identified. Theory, constitutional principles, and constitutional standards remain directionally coherent.

### No Dependency Violations

No dependency violations were identified in the recovered corpus records.

### No Circular Constitutional Dependencies

No circular constitutional dependencies were identified in the recovered corpus as recovered and recorded.

### No Identity Conflicts

No identity conflicts were identified among the recovered artifacts.

### No Lifecycle Conflicts

No lifecycle conflicts were identified between recovered standing and constitutional lifecycle semantics.

### No Metadata Conflicts

No metadata conflicts were identified within the recovered publication context established by Phase 5A.

### No Publication Conflicts

No publication conflicts were identified among publication readiness, registry readiness, validation readiness, and audit readiness.

## Consistency Decision

The recovered constitutional corpus is internally consistent and suitable for certification as Genesis Constitutional Release 1.0.
