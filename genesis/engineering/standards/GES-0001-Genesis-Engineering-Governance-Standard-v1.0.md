# GES-0001 — Genesis Engineering Governance Standard v1.0

Status: Draft
Classification: Constitutional Standard

## Purpose
Establish the mandatory engineering governance framework for all Genesis artifacts.

## Scope
Applies to constitutions, standards, specifications, engineering packages, source code, tests, deployments, and AI-generated artifacts.

## Engineering Hierarchy
1. Constitution
2. Engineering Standards
3. Specifications
4. Engineering Packages
5. Implementations
6. Runtime

## Core Principles
- Engineering SHALL derive from constitutional authority.
- Standards SHALL govern specifications.
- Specifications SHALL govern engineering packages.
- Engineering packages SHALL govern implementations.
- Implementations SHALL remain traceable to governing specifications.

## Artifact Lifecycle
Draft → Review → Approved → Published → Active → Deprecated → Retired → Archived

## Requirement Language
Normative requirements use SHALL, SHALL NOT, MUST, MUST NOT, SHOULD, SHOULD NOT, and MAY.

## Traceability
Every implementation SHALL trace to an engineering package, specification, applicable standards, and constitutional authority.

## Conformance
An artifact conforms to GES-0001 only if it follows this governance model, maintains traceability, and completes the required review and approval lifecycle.
