# Genesis Business Language

This document defines the canonical business vocabulary for Genesis OS.

## Core Principles

- Use business terms before technical terms.
- Prefer clear enterprise language over implementation jargon.
- Preserve meaning across modules, services, workflows, and automation.
- Treat business language as a platform standard, not a local application convention.

## Core Business Terms

- Company: the operating entity that owns business activity.
- Customer: the external party connected to business relationships.
- Project: a coordinated effort with scope, timeline, and outcomes.
- Quote: a proposed commercial offer.
- Order: a committed commercial transaction.
- Task: an atomic unit of work.
- Document: a business record or artifact.
- Capability: a reusable business ability provided by Genesis OS.

## Language Standard

Every platform concept should be named in a way that is understandable to business users, operators, and engineers.
