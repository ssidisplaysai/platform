# Genesis Business Language

The Genesis Business Language is the canonical vocabulary of Genesis OS.

It defines the shared terms, naming patterns, and taxonomy used across runtime, domain, services, modules, workflows, and documentation.

## Purpose

- Establish a consistent platform vocabulary.
- Make business concepts understandable across teams.
- Ensure naming is aligned with enterprise architecture.
- Prevent drift between business language and implementation.

## Scope

This language covers:

- Business terminology
- Object naming
- Relationship naming
- Event naming
- Lifecycle naming
- Status naming
- Capability naming

## Governing Principle

Business language must describe the enterprise domain clearly, consistently, and durably.
