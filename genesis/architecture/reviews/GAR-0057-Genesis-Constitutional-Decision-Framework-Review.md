## 6. Constitutional Definition Assessment

Determination: PASS.

Decision is a governed constitutional determination.

GCSA-0011 defines Decision as a governed constitutional determination that resolves a defined question by selecting, rejecting, authorizing, prioritizing, classifying, constraining, or establishing an outcome from admissible alternatives under explicit authority, criteria, evidence, policy, constraints, and applicability.

GCSA-0011 defines Decision as a governed constitutional determination and separately states that Decision shall own the constitutional meaning of determination while not owning execution or implementation.
