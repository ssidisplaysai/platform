The purpose of this baseline is to define a single constitutional architectural foundation that future Genesis architecture references for alignment, traceability, and governed evolution.

## 3. Scope

This baseline governs constitutional architectural identity, included foundation artifacts, included governance reviews, conformance requirements, dependency declaration obligations, and baseline-change controls.

## 4. Baseline Authority

Baseline authority derives from the Genesis Constitution, under governing intent from GAV-0001 and governing structural framework from GAF-0001.

Genesis Architecture Baseline 1.0 is the proposed canonical constitutional foundation for future Genesis architecture, subject to successful independent re-review and approval.

Approval of a future artifact does not automatically alter this baseline. A governed revision or later baseline publication is required.

## 13. Governance Requirements

Changes to the baseline shall occur only through review, correction, approval, freeze, and publication.

Baseline stability is maintained by controlled governance transitions, immutable approval lineage records, and explicit conformance obligations for new architectural artifacts.
