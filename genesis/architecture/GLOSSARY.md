# Architectural Glossary

Status: Approved
Classification: Genesis Standard
Type: Canonical Glossary

## Reality

The enterprise world as it exists independent of software representation.

## Observation

A recorded noticing of a phenomenon in reality or execution context.

## Evidence

A traceable artifact or record that supports or challenges a claim.

## Knowledge

An evidence-grounded claim with context, confidence, and lineage.

## Truth

The most defensible current model of reality given available verified evidence.

## Verification

The process of testing claims against explicit criteria for acceptance or rejection.

## Semantics

Canonical meaning structures and derivation rules governing concept interpretation.

## Enterprise Genome

The structured canonical representation of enterprise identity, capabilities, relationships, policies, and history.

## Blueprint

A projection of validated enterprise structure into implementation-oriented design form.

## Projection

A downstream representation derived from an upstream canonical model.

## Runtime

The operational environment where projected systems execute and produce outcomes.

## Living Enterprise

The continuous learning loop in which execution outcomes produce new observations for governed evolution.

## Compiler

A deterministic transformation system that converts governed inputs into validated intermediate representations.

## Specification

A governed document defining stable rules, constraints, and interfaces independent of implementation.

## Research

A governed process for testing hypotheses and producing evidence-backed findings.

## Discovery

A validated research outcome with documented evidence, confidence, and limitations.

## Identity

The continuity of an enterprise entity across change, preserved through lineage and invariant constraints.

## Capability

A repeatable enterprise ability to achieve a class of outcomes.

## Relationship

A meaningful connection between entities with defined semantic constraints.

## Policy

A governed rule or constraint that shapes acceptable enterprise behavior.

## Event

An occurrence over time that changes or confirms enterprise state.
