## 1. Purpose

AFR-0007 is the official Architecture Freeze Record for Genesis Constitutional Release 1.0.
It certifies that the recovered Genesis Constitutional Corpus and the constitutional publication framework have reached sufficient maturity, consistency, traceability, governance stability, and publication readiness to become the authoritative constitutional baseline of the Genesis Enterprise Semantic Computing Platform.

This freeze preserves Genesis Constitutional Release 1.0 as the canonical constitutional governance substrate for future architectural, engineering, runtime, and implementation work.
Future work may extend, implement, compile, validate, and operationalize this constitutional baseline, but must not redesign or contradict the frozen constitutional baseline without formal governance approval.

## 4. Frozen Constitutional Principles

Genesis Constitutional Release 1.0 is frozen under the following principles:

1. Constitutional identity is immutable.
2. Constitutional authority flows downward.
3. Dependencies flow toward higher constitutional authority.
4. Constitutional meaning must not change during recovery or publication.
5. Publication preserves identity and lineage.
6. Registry preserves constitutional standing.
7. Validation preserves constitutional semantics.
8. Repository truth is canonical constitutional truth.
9. Review, publication, certification, and freeze remain governance-bound.
10. Future implementation must remain subordinate to the frozen constitutional baseline.

Interpretation of frozen principles:

1. Lower authority may implement or elaborate but may not redefine higher authority.
2. Recovery and certification preserve existing constitutional meaning rather than creating new doctrine.
3. Future automation must execute these frozen meanings, not reinterpret them.
4. Constitutional drift after freeze is a governance defect.
