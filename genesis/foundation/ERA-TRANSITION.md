## Boundary Intent

Foundation artifacts define immutable principles, governance, language, and architecture.

Compiler Platform artifacts define engineering capabilities and compiler behavior.

GCS-0001, GEP-0001, and GSG-0001 are Era II Compiler Platform artifacts and are excluded from Foundation Baseline certification.

Exclusion from Foundation certification does not approve or freeze GCS-0001, GEP-0001, or GSG-0001.
