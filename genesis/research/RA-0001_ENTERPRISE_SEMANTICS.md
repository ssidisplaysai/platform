# RA-0001 Enterprise Semantics

Status: Active
Classification: Genesis Research Area

## Purpose

Study the universal semantic structure of enterprises and determine whether a minimal seed can express enterprise meaning across domains without unstable expansion.

## Core Questions

- What are the smallest irreducible concepts required to describe an enterprise?
- Can all business concepts derive from the Semantic Seed?
- What distinguishes universal concepts from industry extensions?
- How does meaning remain stable across different businesses?

## Current Hypotheses

- H1: The five seed primitives are sufficient for first-order enterprise semantic derivation.
- H2: Most semantic conflicts arise from relationship qualification, not primitive insufficiency.
- H3: Stable semantic meaning requires lineage-preserving derivation discipline.

## Evidence and Experiment Focus

- Comparative derivations across industries using seed primitives.
- Failure analysis where derivation ambiguity appears.
- Repeatability checks for semantic interpretation across independent reviewers.
