## Definition

An evidence-grounded claim about Things, Actors, Relationships, or Events.

## Purpose

Define claim semantics that can be trusted, validated, and governed.

## Parent Concept

None (Seed Primitive)

## Child Concepts

Open for derivation through governance.

## Required Characteristics

- claim statement
- evidence references
- verification or confidence state

## Optional Characteristics

- counterevidence references
- review history
- challenge status

## Allowed Relationships

- refers to Thing, Actor, Relationship, or Event
- may be promoted through governance lifecycle
- may constrain derived semantic proposals

## Evidence Requirements

Evidence bundle must be traceable, reproducible, and sufficient for verification.

## Compiler Rules

Compiler specifications should enforce evidence linkage and deterministic lineage propagation.
No implementation rule is introduced by this document.

## Extension Rules

Derived knowledge concepts must preserve evidence-grounded claim semantics.
