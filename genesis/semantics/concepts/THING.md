# THING

## Canonical ID

GBS-SEED-THING-0001

## Canonical Name

Thing

## Version

1.0.0

## Status

Approved

## Semantic Maturity

Approved

## Definition

Any identifiable unit of reality that can be observed, referenced, and reasoned about.

## Purpose

Provide the universal base semantic for identity-bearing entities.

## Parent Concept

None (Seed Primitive)

## Child Concepts

Open for derivation through governance.

## Required Characteristics

- stable identity
- observational boundary

## Optional Characteristics

- attributes
- state
- classifications

## Allowed Relationships

- may be source or target of Relationship
- may participate in Event context
- may be subject of Knowledge claims

## Evidence Requirements

Evidence must demonstrate existence with source traceability and lineage.

## Compiler Rules

Compiler specifications should enforce deterministic identity mapping and lineage preservation.
No implementation rule is introduced by this document.

## Extension Rules

Derived concepts must preserve Thing identity semantics and may only add constraints.

- asset
