# Naming Conventions

## General

- Use descriptive, domain-oriented names.
- Prefer clear nouns and verbs over abbreviations.
- Use consistent casing across the codebase.

## Examples

- Services: `CustomerService`, `ProjectWorkflowService`
- Repositories: `CustomerRepository`, `ProjectRepository`
- Entities: `Customer`, `ProjectTask`
- Runtime modules: `MetadataRuntime`, `PluginManager`
