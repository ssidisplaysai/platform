- Purpose: Establish immutable constitutional and architectural ground truth for Genesis.
- Current status: Established baseline.
- Primary responsibility: Preserve core authority, invariants, and identity boundaries.

## Genesis Architecture Charter

- Purpose: Define the enduring philosophical and architectural identity of Genesis.
- Current status: Draft.
- Primary responsibility: Preserve meaning, direction, and architectural continuity.

## Genesis Engineering System

- Purpose: Coordinate enterprise engineering as a governed, deterministic discipline.
- Current status: Emerging.
- Primary responsibility: Transform canonical knowledge into traceable engineering outcomes.

## Genesis Development Kit

- Purpose: Provide reusable engineering capabilities for artifact creation and lifecycle flow.
- Current status: Active development.
- Primary responsibility: Enable consistent engineering workflows across teams.

## Knowledge Platform

- Purpose: Establish canonical enterprise understanding from discovery and evidence.
- Current status: In definition.
- Primary responsibility: Preserve truth, context, and knowledge lineage.

## Compiler Platform

- Purpose: Realize engineered intent through controlled transformation into executable forms.
- Current status: In definition.
- Primary responsibility: Maintain deterministic realization from engineered artifacts.

## Runtime Platform

- Purpose: Operate enterprise applications and services with observable behavior.
- Current status: In definition.

## Enterprise Platform

- Purpose: Deliver enterprise capabilities as coherent business-facing systems.
- Current status: In definition.
- Primary responsibility: Align operations with governed enterprise intent.

## Mission Control

- Purpose: Provide cross-platform oversight, observability, and lifecycle coordination.
- Current status: In definition.
- Primary responsibility: Maintain visibility, stewardship, and continuity across Genesis.
