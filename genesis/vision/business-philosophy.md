# Genesis Business Philosophy

Genesis models business meaning as a first-class platform asset.

## Principles

1. Model the business once.
2. Compile implementation artifacts from canonical definitions.
3. Preserve enterprise meaning, traceability, and auditability.
4. Favor long-term platform integrity over short-term convenience.
