# Genesis OS Vision

Genesis OS is the enterprise operating system for connected businesses. It provides a shared runtime, shared domain model, and modular business capabilities that can scale across companies, processes, and domains.

## Long-Term Vision

- One platform for mission control, CRM, projects, manufacturing, inventory, finance, and AI operations.
- Metadata-driven definitions for entities, workflows, rules, and automation.
- A runtime that boots, validates, registers, and orchestrates platform behavior consistently.

## Success Criteria

- New modules can be added without replacing the platform core.
- Business logic remains extensible and testable.
- Metadata changes drive behavior safely.
